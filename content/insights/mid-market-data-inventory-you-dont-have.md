Most mid-market companies cannot answer a simple question:

Where does our data actually live?

Not conceptually. Practically.

## The Reality

Operational data is typically spread across:

- ERP systems
- CRM platforms
- spreadsheets
- department-level tools
- manual processes
- individual workarounds

Each one holds a version of the truth.

None of them are fully aligned.

## Why This Matters

AI systems rely on:

- consistent inputs
- defined structure
- accessible data

Without that:

- agents don’t know which source to trust
- outputs conflict across functions
- decisions become slower, not faster

## The Hidden Risk

Most teams assume:

“We have the data somewhere.”

That’s not enough.

If you don’t know:

- where it is
- how it’s structured
- who owns it

You don’t have usable data.

## What an Actual Data Inventory Looks Like

A real inventory answers:

- What systems hold operational data
- What each system is used for
- Where duplication exists
- Which source is authoritative
- Where data is manually adjusted

This is not a technical exercise.

It’s an operational one.

## Bottom Line

If you don’t know where your data lives:

AI won’t fix that.

It will make the gaps visible — and harder to ignore.
